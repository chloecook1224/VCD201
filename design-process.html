Digital Mockups

Using Adobe Illustator, I started making digital wireframes, first focusing on a logo for the site. I focused on readability, hierarchy, and experimenting with many color themes and type fonts in order to figure out what I wanted to desgin.

Refined Sketches

I made sketches to design the rest of the webbpage layouts to refine spacing, balance, and interaction areas. The hand-drawn aspect helped me think more freely before committing to a digital design.

Alternate Layouts

This is my alternative idea for a sketch wirefram for my site to see how navigation and images, etc could be structured dynamically across the screen.

Initial Design

I began coding a loose idea of my drawings and mockups. I then started expermenting with the code and changing some ideas along the way as I wasnt fully satisfied with the look. This helped me narrow down a look that felt both cyberpunk-inspired and user-friendly.

Final Design

The final layout uses a glowing nav system triggered by the button or title (VCD201). Adding a click me visual allowed for users to smoothly move through the experience.
