VCD201 Project 1 by Chloe Cook

This project explores the intersection of visual communication and design thinking, showcasing a unique process from initial research to final creative execution. Rooted in contemporary visual culture, the work blends bold aesthetics with thoughtful design choices, drawing inspiration from cyberpunk themes and urban street style. Through this site, you’ll find detailed insights into the research behind the concept, the evolution of the design process, and a curated style guide that informs the final product. Whether you're here for inspiration or curiosity, take a look around — and enjoy the journey behind the design.
